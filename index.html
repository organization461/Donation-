Our Mission & Vision

Hope Haven was established in 2012 as a response to the rising number of abandoned infants in our community. We believe that every child, regardless of their circumstances, deserves to be held, loved, and given a chance to thrive.


Our facility provides 24-hour pediatric nursing care, early childhood development classes, and a robust adoption program that connects children with their forever families.

Gift Card Donations

Donating a gift card is a safe and instant way to help us buy formula, medicine, and diapers from international retailers.


  • Amazon & Walmart: For supplies and food.
  • Apple & iTunes: For educational software.
  • Visa/Mastercard: For emergency medical bills.

Your data is protected. By uploading an image, our procurement team can verify and redeem the funds instantly to avoid delays in care.
